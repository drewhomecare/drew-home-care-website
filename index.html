What We Offer

Our Comprehensive Non-Medical Services

A full range of personalized services to help your loved one thrive safely and comfortably at home.

Assistance with Daily Living

Support with everyday activities to help seniors maintain their routines and independence with confidence.

Personal Care Services

Dignified assistance with bathing, grooming, dressing, and other personal hygiene needs.

Companionship & Social Support

Meaningful companionship to reduce isolation, stimulate the mind, and bring joy to each day.

Medication Reminders

Timely reminders to ensure medications are taken as prescribed for health and peace of mind.

Meal Preparation & Nutrition

Nutritious, home-cooked meals tailored to dietary preferences and health needs.

Light Housekeeping

A clean, comfortable home environment — laundry, tidying, and organizing to support daily well-being.

Transportation Services

Safe, reliable transportation to appointments, errands, social events, and community activities.

Home Safety Evaluations

A thorough review of the home environment to identify and address potential safety concerns.

Our Story

Who We Are & Our Mission

After more than 20 years as a mechanical engineer, I left my corporate career to pursue more meaningful work. My family's experience caring for my father-in-law with Alzheimer's, and now supporting my mother with dementia, shaped my commitment to this work.

I became a Certified Nursing Assistant and a Residential Care Facility for the Elderly Administrator to better understand how to provide compassionate, high-quality care. At Drew Home Care, I am dedicated to providing dependable care with the dignity every family deserves.

— Andrew Chang, Ph.D., Owner

Our Mission
